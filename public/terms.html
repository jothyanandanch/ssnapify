Frequently Asked Questions

How do credits work?

Credits are used to perform AI transformations on your images. Different tools consume different amounts of credits:

  • AI Restore, Background Removal, Object Removal, Enhancement: 1 credit each
  • Background Replacement: 2 credits
  • Generative Fill: 3 credits

Credits reset monthly on your billing date.

What image formats are supported?

We support all major image formats including:

  • JPEG (.jpg, .jpeg)
  • PNG (.png)
  • GIF (.gif)
  • WebP (.webp)

Maximum file size is 10MB per image.

How can I upgrade my plan?

You can upgrade your plan anytime from the Pricing page. Choose between:

  • Pro Monthly: $9.99/month for 500 credits
  • Pro 6-Month: $49.99/6 months for 500 credits per month (17% savings)

Upgrades are effective immediately and unused credits carry over.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We take your privacy seriously:

  • Images are processed securely using Cloudinary's infrastructure
  • We don't share your images with third parties
  • You can delete your images anytime
  • We use Google OAuth for secure authentication

How long does processing take?

Processing times vary by transformation type:

  • Basic enhancements: 5-15 seconds
  • Background removal/replacement: 10-30 seconds
  • Generative fill: 30-60 seconds

Processing may take longer during peak usage times.
