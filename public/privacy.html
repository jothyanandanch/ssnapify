Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

Information We Collect

When you use SSnapify, we collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google OAuth, we collect your email address and display name.
  • Images: Images you upload for processing are temporarily stored on our secure servers.
  • Usage Data: We track how you use our service to improve functionality and user experience.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser information, and device identifiers for security and analytics.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve our AI image processing services
  • Manage your account and billing
  • Communicate with you about service updates
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

Image Processing and Storage

Image Security: Your images are processed using Cloudinary's secure infrastructure. Images are encrypted in transit and at rest.

Retention: Processed images are stored in your account until you delete them. Original images may be temporarily cached for processing.

AI Processing: Images are processed using cloud-based AI services. We do not use your images to train AI models.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Service Providers: Cloudinary for image processing, Google for authentication
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger or acquisition

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Export your data
  • Object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ssnapify.com

Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Monitoring for suspicious activity

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us:

  • Email: privacy@ssnapify.com
  • Support: Visit our Support Center
